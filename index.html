Game Overview

2D tile-based casual game; collect treasure as you explore the forest. Navigate the land and overcome obstacles to harvest all of the mushrooms. Solve puzzles to collect the rare garnets. Be sure to talk to everyone if you want a shot at claiming the rewards on offer.

Controls

  • Arrow Keys - player movement / menu navigation
  • Spacebar - perform action / select option
  • Enter - pause menu
